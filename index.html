The Woven Anatolia Live ↗

A Shopify webshop for a new entrepreneur selling authentic Anatolian rugs. The project started with UX research into the rug retail category to understand user expectations and define the sitemap and key user flows. I designed the full interface in Figma and developed the site on Shopify's Dawn theme, building a custom frontend and rebuilding several components from scratch to fit the brand. The backend used Dawn's templates with a few custom components added where the client's catalog required it. Technical SEO was a priority throughout the build, and I also provided the client with guidance on organic marketing to support growth after launch.
